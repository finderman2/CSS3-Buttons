CSS3 Buttons

This is a small collection of buttons that show what is possible using CSS3, while also maintaining the simplest possible markup. They look best in WebKit-based browsers, almost as good in Firefox, with all other browsers falling back to a less-styled button. If you use these buttons in a project, or just want to say hi drop me a dime.
